Admin/ Sales Manager :
Role Description

The role of the Sales Manager is to guide and lead the sales team to success. the most important of these goals is to achieve or overachieve on sales targets. Second to this the role requires confidence, leadership and experience. To enable and support each seller so that they can best perform and achive their own duties. Leading by example is the best practice for this.

Salesperson Permissions

The Admin/ Manager has the same permissions as a seller with the additoinal functoinality of being able to add and remove salespeolpe to and from the team. Therefore the manager can create, read, update and delete new users as the team changes in size.

Same as a seller, career progression is not guaranteed or gifted, this is awarded to those who do exxceptional work and who cimmit themselves and their efforts to the success of the team. A manager looking to be promoted to a Director must lead by example, have a proven track record of reaching targets, be creative by bringing new approaches and fresh ideas to the Senior Leadership team. In doing this, the conversatoin then changes from what the company can do for the manager, to what the manager can do for the company growth.

Bonuses & Pay

There is a competetive bonus structure and commission plan to keep you motivated! Sales Managers are paid differently to sellers as they are not responsible for just one sale they are responsible for all sales of the team and the overall team target. Therefore they receive an agreed upon higher base salary as well as quaterly and annual bonuses for achieving the agreed upon target.
